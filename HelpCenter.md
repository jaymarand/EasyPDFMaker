# Help Center

**Welcome to Image To PDF Maker Help**

Find answers to common questions and learn how to get the most out of the app.

## Getting Started

### How do I convert images to PDF?

1. Tap the **+** button on the home screen
2. Choose **Select from Photos** or **Take Photo**
3. Select one or more images
4. Review and arrange your images if needed
5. Tap **Create PDF** to generate your document
6. Your PDF is ready to view, share, or save!

### What image formats are supported?

Image To PDF Maker supports all common image formats including:
- JPEG/JPG
- PNG
- HEIC (iOS photos)
- GIF
- TIFF
- BMP

### Where are my PDFs saved?

All PDFs are stored locally on your device. You can access them from:
- The home screen of the app
- Your device's Files app (if you save them there)
- Any location you choose when sharing or exporting

## Working with Images

### How do I combine multiple images into one PDF?

When selecting images, you can choose multiple photos at once. The app will automatically combine them into a single PDF document with each image as a separate page.

### Can I rearrange pages in my PDF?

Yes! After selecting your images, you can drag and drop them to reorder the pages before creating your PDF.

### How do I crop or edit images before converting?

Currently, images are converted as-is. For best results, crop and edit your images using the Photos app before importing them into Image To PDF Maker.

### What's the best image quality for PDFs?

For most documents:
- Use clear, well-lit photos
- Ensure text is readable
- Higher resolution images produce better quality PDFs
- JPEG quality of 80-90% provides a good balance of quality and file size

## PDF Features

### How do I add a signature to my PDF?

1. Go to **Settings** > **General** > **Add Signature**
2. Draw your signature on the screen
3. Save your signature
4. When creating or viewing a PDF, tap the signature icon
5. Place your signature where you want it on the document

### Can I password protect my PDFs?

Password protection is a planned feature for future updates. Currently, PDFs are created without password protection.

### How do I delete a signature?

1. Go to **Settings** > **General** > **View Signature**
2. Tap the **Delete** button to remove your saved signature

## Sharing and Exporting

### How do I share my PDF?

1. Open the PDF you want to share
2. Tap the **Share** button
3. Choose how you want to share:
   - Email
   - Messages
   - AirDrop
   - Save to Files
   - Other sharing options

### Can I save PDFs to cloud storage?

Yes! When sharing or exporting a PDF, you can choose to save it to:
- iCloud Drive
- Google Drive
- Dropbox
- OneDrive
- Any other cloud storage app installed on your device

### What file format are the exported documents?

All documents are exported as standard PDF files that can be opened on any device with a PDF reader.

## Scanning Documents

### How do I use the document scanner?

1. Tap the **+** button on the home screen
2. Select **Scan Document**
3. Position your camera over the document
4. The app will automatically detect edges and capture
5. Adjust corners if needed
6. Tap **Keep Scan** to add more pages or **Save** to finish

### Why isn't the scanner detecting my document?

Make sure:
- You have good lighting
- The document is on a contrasting background
- The entire document is visible in the camera frame
- The document is flat and not wrinkled

## Managing PDFs

### How do I delete a PDF?

1. Long-press on the PDF in your library
2. Tap **Delete** from the menu
3. Confirm deletion

Alternatively, swipe left on a PDF and tap the delete button.

### How do I rename a PDF?

1. Long-press on the PDF
2. Tap **Rename**
3. Enter the new name
4. Tap **Done**

### Can I organize PDFs into folders?

Folder organization is a planned feature for future updates. Currently, all PDFs are displayed in a single library view.

## Troubleshooting

### The app won't open my images

Make sure:
- You've granted Photo Library access in Settings
- Your images are in a supported format
- You have enough storage space on your device
- The app is up to date

### My PDF looks blurry or low quality

To improve quality:
- Use higher resolution source images
- Ensure images are in focus when taking photos
- Use good lighting when scanning documents
- Check that images aren't over-compressed

### The app crashed or froze

Try these steps:
1. Force quit the app and reopen it
2. Restart your device
3. Check for app updates in the App Store
4. Clear some storage space if your device is low on storage

If problems persist, contact our support team.

### PDFs aren't exporting

Check that:
- You have enough storage space
- You've granted necessary permissions
- The destination app or service is working properly
- Your device has an active internet connection (for cloud services)

## Privacy and Security

### Does the app upload my images or PDFs?

No! All image processing and PDF creation happens locally on your device. Your images and documents never leave your device unless you explicitly choose to share or upload them.

### What data does the app collect?

We collect minimal data to improve the app. See our Privacy Policy for complete details. Your images and documents are never collected or stored by us.

### Is my data secure?

Yes! All your images and PDFs are stored locally on your device, protected by your device's security features.

## Tips and Best Practices

### For Scanning Documents

- Use a flat, contrasting surface
- Ensure even lighting (avoid shadows)
- Keep the camera steady
- Scan one page at a time for best results

### For Photo Quality

- Clean your camera lens
- Use good lighting
- Hold your device steady
- Take photos straight-on (not at an angle)

### For File Organization

- Name your PDFs descriptively
- Use the share function to save important PDFs to cloud storage
- Regularly back up important documents

## Updates and New Features

### How do I update the app?

1. Open the App Store
2. Tap your profile icon
3. Scroll to see pending updates
4. Tap **Update** next to Image To PDF Maker

Or enable automatic updates in your device settings.

### What features are coming soon?

We're constantly working to improve the app. Planned features include:
- Folder organization

- Advanced editing tools
- OCR (text recognition)
