Privacy Policy for Easy PDF Maker

Last updated: March 04, 2026

Introduction

At Easy PDF Maker, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Our Commitment to Privacy

Easy PDF Maker is designed with your privacy in mind. The app processes all images and documents locally on your device. We do not upload your photos, images, or documents to external servers or cloud storage unless you explicitly choose to share the converted PDFs through third-party services.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • **Content**: Images, photos, and documents you upload to convert to PDF format are processed locally on your device
  • **Optional Information**: If you contact us for support, you may provide your email address and any information you choose to include in your message

Information Automatically Collected

  • **Device Information**: We may collect information about your device, including device model, operating system version, and unique device identifiers
  • **Usage Information**: We may collect information about how you use the app, such as features accessed and actions performed
  • **Crash Reports**: Anonymous crash reports and diagnostic information to help us improve app stability

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and maintain the app's functionality
  • Process your images and documents locally on your device
  • Respond to your support requests and inquiries
  • Improve and optimize the app's performance
  • Identify and fix technical issues
  • Understand how users interact with the app to enhance user experience

On-Device Processing

All image and document processing occurs locally on your device. Your content is:

  • **Not uploaded** to our servers or cloud storage
  • **Not shared** with third parties unless you explicitly choose to do so
  • **Stored only on your device** and under your control
  • **Automatically deleted** when you remove converted PDFs or uninstall the app

Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties, except in the following circumstances:

With Your Consent

When you explicitly choose to share or export PDFs through third-party services (such as email, messaging apps, or cloud storage), those services are governed by their own privacy policies.

Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required to do so by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities, such as:

  • Complying with legal obligations
  • Protecting and defending our rights or property
  • Preventing or investigating possible wrongdoing
  • Protecting the personal safety of users or the public

Service Providers

We may share anonymous usage data with trusted service providers who assist us in operating the app, conducting our business, or serving our users, provided that those parties agree to keep this information confidential.

Data Storage and Security

Local Storage

All user content (images, photos, documents, and PDFs) is stored locally on your device. We implement appropriate security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction.

Security Measures

  • Data encryption in transit when communicating with our servers (for app updates and support only)
  • Secure coding practices to prevent vulnerabilities
  • Regular security assessments and updates

Despite our efforts, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your information, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.
