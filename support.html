Basic Help

How do I create a PDF?
Tap Scan Document or import an image or file, review the pages, and save your PDF.

How do I sign a document?
Open a saved PDF and use the signature tool to place your saved signature on the page.

Does the app upload my documents?
No. Documents are processed locally on your device unless you explicitly share or export them.
